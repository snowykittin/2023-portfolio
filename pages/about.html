Who am I?

A jack of many trades

My name is Summer, but you may see me online under the alias snowykittin.

I've been programming as long as I've been able to be on the internet. It started with editing premade HTML templates, then went into working on BBCode on forumotion forms. I finally got serious about coding in high school, and haven't stopped since. I'm also a community manager, moderating Discord communities on the side. Behind the screen, I'm a violinist, cat nerd, fiction writer, and soon-to-be wife.

Fun Fact: I had sworn since I was a little girl that I would go into medicine and become a neurologist. Clearly, that plan has changed!
